# Neon Effects Module

## Overview
The **Effects Module** provides spatial and textural enhancement for the final signal. Designed to be clean and punchy, these effects provide the "Neon Polish."

## Effect Types

### 1. Delay (Neon Echo)
- **Style:** Digital delay with high-pass/low-pass damping.
- **Features:** Ping-pong mode and tempo sync.

### 2. Reverb (Neon Space)
- **Style:** Algorithmic hall/plate simulator.
- **Features:** "Freeze" button for infinite tails.

### 3. Chorus/Flanger (Neon Pulse)
- **Style:** BBD-style modulation.
- **Features:** Wide stereo spread.

## Parameter Cards
- **Effect Type:** (Toggle) Cycle through Delay, Reverb, Chorus.
- **Mix/Dry-Wet:** (Linear) Balance between source and effect.
- **Time/Rate:** (Linear) Speed of modulation or delay feedback interval.
- **Feedback/Depth:** (Linear) Intensity of the effect.
- **Tone/Color:** (Linear) Frequency bias (Dark to Bright).
- **Width:** (Linear) Stereo intensification.
- **Sync/Free:** (Toggle) Tempo alignment.
- **Mode:** (Toggle) Specific sub-mode (e.g., Ping-pong, Hall/Room).

## Visual Representation (Top 2/3)
- **Procedural Art:** Stylized animation representing the effect (e.g., repeating echo rings or a growing "Cloud" for reverb).
- **Color:** **Sky Blue (`0xff0088ff`)**.
